CHERISH 2023

1. PHONE BOOTH:
● All the students were made into the groups each group consists of 5 members
● Among that 5 members 4 are grouped and one member will be aside to ring the bell from each group
● After that member rings bell so that he can answer
● For each and every group we will ask some questions time is alocated 10-15 seconds upon those questions who gives more answer will be first they will be winner of that particular round
● In the same way 5 rounds will be conducted with the 5 different questions
● Winner will be decided on the basics of less time duration and more answers

2. CHINESE WHISPER:
5 to 10 members a team.
long sentence will be given to the first person they should next person these goes on till last person.
In which team the last person will spell the sentence correctly that team will be the winner.

3. MAKEUP WITHOUT LOOKING INTO MIRROR:
A participant is supposed to apply makeup on their own face with Sugar cosmetic products, without using a mirror.

4. LEMON AND SPOON:
Players stand on the start line, each with their lemon resting on their spoon. When you shout go, all players race to the finish line.
If their lemon falls player have to discontinue the game.
To win lemon run you need to cross the line first without dropping your lemon.

5. HEADPHONE GAME:
Two person will be given headphone with full volume
Songs or dialogues will be played with some disturbance.
Participants should guess the line or movie name or songs by lip reading of the other person
Time limit one minute

6. DECORATING PARTNER:
Players should get their partner ready as it is given in the image .
Timing 1 to 5 minutes Based on the images .
Images are given by student co-ordinators.

7. GUESS THE ITEM WITHOUT SEEING:
Timing 30 Seconds
Participant’s eyes must be closed with a cloth.
Participants should identify the object by touching and smelling the objects.

8. FIND THE CONNECTION BETWEEN TWO IMAGES:
Two images will be provided .
Guess the connection between the both images.
Ex:Two pictures one is of hero and another is of heroine participant should guess on what movie they are connected(Acted)

9. FUN FROM JUNK:
Give the participants 60 minutes to collect junk from the college campus.
And in next 30 minutes, they need to make a sellable product from the junk.
It can be a toy, jewellery item, home decor items, something to make a mechanical process easier etc.
The list is endless. They can later do a pitch about the product, and a jury decides which one is the best!

10. GUESS THE MOVIE NAME WITH CLUES:
Atleast 4 player are needed for this game. You are not allowed to talk while making your partner to guess the movie name. One minute time limit of the each acting performed by the member of each team.
